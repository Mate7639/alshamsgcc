Services

Substation

Alshms Group as a EPC Contractor has successfully undertaken and commissioned as per schedule 63 Nos. of 132kV Substation Projects in UAE comprising 430 Nos. of 132kV GIS Bays, 191 Nos. of 132kV Power Transformers up to 315 MVA Capacity and 900 km. of 132kV-220kV Cabling works along with associated MV/LV Switchgears, Distribution Transformers, Control & protection Panels, SCADA Systems, etc. for major utilities like Dubai Electricity & Water Authority (DEWA), Federal Electricity & Water Authority (FEWA, Formerly MEW), Abu Dhabi Water & Electricity Authority (ADWEA) and Dubai Aluminium Company Ltd. (DUBAL).

To meet the growing stringent demands of the Electricity sector, the latest testing equipment and instruments used in this field are possessed by Alshams Group.

The in-house team has been trained to test & commission various types of Gas Insulted Switchgears, Reactors, LVAC & DC Systems, MV Switchgear Circuit Breakers, EHV/HV Transformers, Capacitor Banks various types of Protection Equipments, SCADA and Communication System, etc.

With a staff comprising highly experienced and competent engineers and with computer aided and micro-station design facilities, Alshams group has the expertise to undertake design, co-ordinate, install, test and commission projects in the field of Power Generation, Desalination Projects, EHV Substations and Transmission Lines up to 400kV. Alshams group has offices at key places all over the UAE and this puts Alshams Group in a very advantageous position to closely coordinate with major manufacturers of equipment and consultants.
