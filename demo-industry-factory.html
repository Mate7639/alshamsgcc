More About Us

Founded in 2000, Al Shams has built a strong reputation as a trusted partner in the electrical contracting industry across the UAE. Our expertise lies in delivering high-quality infrastructure and substation projects, completed with precision and a commitment to excellence. Operating in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and the Northern Emirates, we serve a range of industries with a portfolio of successful collaborations in both the public and private sectors.

Our clients trust us because of our proven track record, our focus on quality, and our dedication to delivering on time.
