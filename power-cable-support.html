Power Cable Support and Tray Installation Solutions

At ALSHAMS GROUP, we provide innovative power cable support and tray systems that deliver unparalleled organization, safety, and long-lasting performance. Our expertise ensures your electrical cables are securely supported and routed, minimizing risk, enhancing accessibility, and facilitating easy maintenance. Whether you’re working on a large-scale industrial facility, a commercial project, or a utility upgrade, we have the perfect solutions to keep your electrical infrastructure running smoothly.​

Custom Power Cable Support Systems

  • Heavy-Duty Performance: Designed to handle cables of all sizes and weights, from light-duty to heavy-duty requirements.​
  • Optimized Cable Routing: We ensure a smooth and efficient layout that maximizes space and minimizes cable wear and tear.​
  • Stability and Durability: Secure cable mounting systems that minimize movement, reduce strain, and prolong the life of your cables.​
  • Compliance Guaranteed: All installations meet national and international engineering and safety standards, ensuring reliability and peace of mind.​

Cable Tray and Ladder Installation

  • Strength and Reliability: Built to withstand heavy-duty use, our trays and ladders offer dependable support, even in the most challenging conditions.​
  • Adaptable Layouts: Customizable systems designed to fit your facility’s layout, enabling efficient cable management while allowing for future growth and changes.​
  • Ventilation and Heat Control: Optimized to promote airflow and prevent overheating, which ensures safe and effective cable operation.​
  • Variety of Tray Types: Choose from perforated trays, solid bottom trays, cable ladders, and troughs to best suit your project’s unique requirements.​

​ Whether your project involves a small-scale installation or a complex network, we ensure your cables are managed efficiently for long-term reliability.​

Sleek and Safe Cable Trunking & Raceway Solutions

For spaces that demand a cleaner, more streamlined appearance, we offer cable trunking and raceway systems that deliver organization with style. These solutions provide:

  • Neat and Professional Finish: Our trunking and raceways blend seamlessly into your workspace, offering a tidy, professional look.
  • Protection Against External Elements: Safeguard cables from environmental conditions, physical damage, and interference.
  • Ease of Access: Simplified designs that provide easy access for maintenance, upgrades, and troubleshooting, making your system future-ready.
