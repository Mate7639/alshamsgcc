EL & 110V Battery Installation Works​

At ALSHAMS GROUP, we specialize in the installation of battery banks, charger panels, and Battery Monitoring Systems (BMS) to ensure uninterrupted power and optimal system reliability. Whether it’s an EL (Emergency Lighting) system or a 110V battery backup, we deliver solutions that enhance your system’s resilience and performance.​

Our Expertise Includes:

  • Battery Bank Installation: Proper setup of high-capacity battery systems tailored to your operational needs.
  • Charger Panel Installation: Integration of reliable charger panels to ensure seamless power supply and efficient charging cycles.
  • Battery Monitoring Systems (BMS): Installation of advanced BMS for real-time monitoring of battery health, voltage levels, and performance metrics.
  • Custom Configurations: Designing and installing systems that align with the specific requirements of industrial, commercial, and critical infrastructure applications.

Why Choose Our Battery Installation Services?

  • Precision and Reliability: We ensure all components are installed with utmost precision for long-lasting performance.
  • Safety-First Approach: Our installations comply with all safety standards, ensuring secure and hazard-free operations.
  • Cutting-Edge Technology: Expertise in integrating smart Battery Monitoring Systems for enhanced operational efficiency.
  • End-to-End Support: From initial setup to testing and commissioning, we provide comprehensive services tailored to your needs.

Reliable Power, Anytime, Anywhere. With our EL & 110V Battery Installation Works, you can rest assured that your power systems are in capable hands.
